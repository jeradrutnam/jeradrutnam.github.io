About

18 Years
Shaping the Web

I'm a Frontend Engineer and UI/UX Specialist at WSO2, where I've spent nearly two decades shaping how enterprise software looks, feels, and works. My practice spans design systems, identity infrastructure, and developer experience — always in open source.

Most recently I've led the frontend of ThunderID, an open-source Identity and Access Management platform built to make enterprise security approachable. I believe the best security tools disappear into the workflow.

Beyond shipping products, I design and maintain Font WSO2 and WSO2 Visual Elements — the visual language across WSO2's entire product suite. My career has spanned PaaS infrastructure with Apache Stratos, developer tooling in VS Code, and analytics dashboards.

I hold a Distinction degree from the University of Westminster and speak at React and JavaScript conferences across Asia.
